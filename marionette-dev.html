My role: ui/ux designer

Over the last year, I have been a dedicated UX/UI designer at Marionette.dev, a sophisticated fintech platform that seamlessly integrates cryptocurrency and fiat transactions.

My role involved continuously evolving the user experience and interface through iterative design processes, hypothesis testing, and collaborative decision-making.

In addition to the web application, I also designed a mobile application that mirrors the functionality and aesthetic of the web platform.

Key Responsibilities and Contributions:

Collaborative Design Process

- Collaborated closely with product managers, developers;
- Participated in design sprints and brainstorming sessions to foster innovation and creativity;
- Made visual design decisions as a team, incorporating diverse insights and perspectives.

UI/UX Design and Prototyping

- Created high-fidelity prototypes and wireframes using Figma, enhancing my proficiency with the tool;
- Designed intuitive and visually appealing interfaces that simplified complex blockchain and cryptocurrency concepts;
- Established a cohesive visual design language that maintained consistency across both web and mobile platforms.

Continuous Improvement and Updates:

- Implemented regular updates based on user feedback and analytics to enhance the user experience;
- Stayed abreast of fintech and blockchain trends to keep the platform innovative and competitive;
- Continuously improved my skills in Figma and other design tools through practice and study.
