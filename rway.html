RWAY App

Combines unified solutions to Store, Stake, Invest, Swap assets and Find Investment Opportunities

Industry: RWA, Blockchain, Crypto

Company: RWAY

My role: Product Designer & Web developer

In a nutshell

This project was developed to provide capabilities for storing, staking, investing, swapping assets, and finding investment opportunities.

It was built in collaboration with one of the owners of Marionette.dev. We redesigned existing screens with a primary focus on the mobile app.

RWAY in Figma

When updating screens and user flows, I utilized new Figma tools like Autolayout, Variables, Sections etc. These features weren't available in Figma when I initially worked on Marionette.dev.
